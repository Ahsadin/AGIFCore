# Innovation Rules

## What Counts As Innovation

Innovation in AGIFCore means a bounded improvement that still obeys the frozen master plan, the constitution, and the current phase boundary.

## Phase 1 Rules

- Phase 1 is for governed drafting and validation, not speculative feature expansion.
- A new idea may be recorded only if it is explicitly tied to a frozen master-plan clause.
- A new idea may not change the authority order, phase approval rules, or user approval requirement.
- A new idea may not create hidden scope in requirements, design, or runtime work.
- If an idea belongs to a later phase, park it instead of smuggling it into Phase 1.

## Allowed Form

Allowed innovation in this phase is limited to:

- clearer wording
- tighter scope boundaries
- better evidence organization
- cleaner review flow
