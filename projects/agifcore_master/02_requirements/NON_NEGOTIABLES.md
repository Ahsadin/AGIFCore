# Non-Negotiables

## Phase 1 Rules That Cannot Move

- AGIFCore stays local-first.
- No hidden external model may be treated as runtime truth.
- No cloud-correctness dependency may be required for phase truth.
- No benchmark-id routing may be used as a proxy for intelligence.
- No generic filler may be accepted as intelligence.
- No copied old code may count as done until it is rebuilt, verified, demoed, and approved by the user.
- No agent may self-approve a phase.
- No placeholder file may be counted as closure evidence.
- No later phase may begin until the frozen Phase 1 review chain is complete.

## Constitution Alignment

These rules are enforced through:

- `projects/agifcore_master/01_plan/SYSTEM_CONSTITUTION.md`
- `projects/agifcore_master/02_requirements/PHASE_APPROVAL_RULES.md`
- `projects/agifcore_master/01_plan/PHASE_GATE_CHECKLIST.md`

## Phase 1 Scope

These rules are frozen for Phase 1 drafting and later execution. They do not authorize new features, new roles, or any change to the frozen authority order.
