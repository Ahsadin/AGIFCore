# Conversation Scope

## Scope Boundary

AGIFCore conversation is the user-facing surface for explanation, clarification, planning, comparison, teaching, and bounded synthesis.

## What The Conversation Surface Must Do

- answer plainly when support is strong
- say `clarify`, `search_needed`, `unknown`, or `abstain` when support is weak
- stay honest about uncertainty
- keep language tied to inspectable support

## What It Must Not Do

- pretend that fluent wording is proof of reasoning
- pretend that summary text is proof of phase truth
- hide unresolved blockers behind confidence
- cross into runtime authority or user approval

## Phase 1 Rule

Phase 1 uses this file to freeze the later conversation model boundaries without claiming those later conversation behaviors already exist.
