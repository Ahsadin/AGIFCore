# Claim Boundary Summary

Date: `2026-04-05`
Status: `public_safe_copy`

AGIFCore is published here only as a bounded intelligence architecture/runtime/core.

## What Is Supported

- local system, project, runtime, and evidence questions
- bounded reasoning, simple math, and structured comparison
- follow-up binding from prior-turn state
- contradiction handling and honest fail-closed behavior
- narrow bounded current-world estimates with uncertainty labels

## What Is Not Claimed

- broad open-ended chat intelligence
- unrestricted conversational intelligence
- general AGI
- unrestricted world knowledge
